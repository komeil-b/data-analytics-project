A Summary of My Insights

Overview:
This dashboard analyzes sales and customer activity across multiple store regions and product categories for a randomized retail business. Through it and the use of data generated and modeled through Python, SQL, and Power BI, I can highlight performance trends.

Data Performance Key Findings:

- Total yearly sales show a steady upward trend, with the highest revenue months occurring mid-year.

- Digital channels consistently outperform physical retail locations in both revenue and transaction volume.

- Gift products generate higher average order values, while lottery products drive overall transaction counts.

- Region A and Region C represent the strongest performing markets, this could indicate stronger digital adoption .

- Customer volume growth aligns with promotional periods therefore marketing has had positive impacts on engagement.
